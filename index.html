6 Reasons Why you Need Rose Water

August 26, 2017

Roses are red…

The key to any woman’s heart is a beautiful bouquet roses, or a bed sprinkled with a romantic array of rose petals. But when it comes to clear and gorgeous skin, what’s the secret?

Simple, it’s rose water.

Why has it become such a fad all of a sudden?

Hmm… I actually feel like rose water doesn’t get much recognition yet. Not as much as coconut oil (though I really think that ish is bomb) but let’s make it happen!

Let’s bring this under rated floral potion into the spotlight of every girl’s and boy’s skin care routine. Here’s why…

1. It’s an anti-inflammatory and anti-bacterial solution for puffy eyes and skin problems such as eczema, rosea, dandruff and acne.

2. It’s a gentle and natural. skin-balancing toner. Perfect for removing dirt and oil after a long day.

3. It can act as a refreshing skin mist during hot and humid days or even after your gorgeous make up routine.

4. It smells a-m-a-z-i-n-g and totally helps with tiredness and stress. It’s like an instant mist for anxiety relief.

5. It’s anti-aging since it’s super hydrating and helps maintain your skin’s elasticity. LOOK younger, FEEL younger.

6. It’s filled with many anti-oxidants, vitamins and minerals. Your skin will feel as smooth and supple as a baby’s bum bum.
